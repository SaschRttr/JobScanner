When analyses and dashboards were frequently missing or took weeks to deliver, I independently learned Python, SQL, Databricks and Plotly – reducing delivery times from up to three weeks to an average of two days. My drive is to truly understand things – and from that understanding, create better tools and solutions for myself and my colleagues.
